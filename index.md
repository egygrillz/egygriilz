## What is EGYGRILLZ?

EGYGRILLZ is Egypt’s first and only custom dental jewelry studio, founded in 2021 by Dr. Mohamed Mahdy. Every set of grillz is designed around your unique smile — combining centuries-old goldsmithing tradition with a bold, contemporary edge.

We are not a general jewelry store. We specialize exclusively in custom dental jewelry — removable pieces fitted precisely to your teeth using dental impression techniques.

## Our Story

In 2021, EGYGRILLZ emerged as Egypt’s first premium grillz brand, transforming traditional dental accessories into wearable works of art. We redefined what grillz represent in Egypt — moving beyond a cultural symbol to become exclusive jewelry suitable for every occasion.

Notable clients and brand endorsers include **Marwan Pablo**, **Maged El Kedwany**, and **Abou El Anwar** — confirming EGYGRILLZ as a symbol of legacy in Egypt’s artistic world.

The brand is expanding beyond grillz into unique one-of-a-kind pieces including custom chess sets and functional art, positioning EGYGRILLZ as a creative force blurring the lines between jewelry and fine art.
