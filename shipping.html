Care & Shipping

How to maintain your piece in perfect condition, and how we deliver it to you.

Official Carrier: FedEx  ·  Alternative shipping is at the client's sole risk

Part I

Maintenance & Care

01
Rinse After Every WearClean with cold water immediately after removing your grillz.
02
Soft Brush OnlyUse a soft-bristle toothbrush for stubborn buildup — never hard-bristle or metal tools.
03
No Abrasive ToothpasteNever use whitening or abrasive toothpaste on gold-plated items.
04
Dry ThoroughlyPat dry with a microfiber cloth before storing. Moisture causes tarnishing over time.
05
EGYGRILLZ Case OnlyAlways store your piece in the provided protective case.
06
No ChemicalsNever expose to cleaners, bleach, chlorine, perfumes, hairspray, or colognes.
07
No Eating or SleepingRemove your grillz before eating, drinking anything hot or acidic, or going to sleep.
08
No Smoking While WearingSmoking causes accelerated tarnishing and discoloration.
09
Avoid Heat & Direct SunlightKeep away from high heat sources or prolonged direct sunlight.
10
Check Stones WeeklyInspect stones for looseness every week.
11
Do Not ShareNever share grillz with another person. Each piece is custom-fitted.
12
Monthly Deep CleanDeep clean once a month using a professional jewelry-specific cleaning solution.
13
Oral Hygiene FirstAlways brush and floss your natural teeth thoroughly before inserting your grillz.
14
Handle with CareDo not use your grillz to open bottles or bite down on hard objects.
15
No Harsh Heat ExposureDo not steam clean or place near open flame.
