The Legacy

In 2021, EgyGrillz emerged as Egypt’s first premium grillz brand, transforming traditional dental accessories into wearable works of art. We redefined what grillz represent—moving beyond a cultural symbol to become exclusive jewelry suitable for every occasion.

Our commitment to luxury and precision attracted cultural pioneers including Marwan Pablo, Maged El Kedwany, and Abou El Anwar. Their endorsement confirmed that EgyGrillz has become a symbol of legacy in the artistic world.

Looking forward, we are expanding our horizons with one-of-a-kind pieces like custom chess sets and functional art, positioning EgyGrillz as a creative force that blurs the lines between jewelry and fine art.
