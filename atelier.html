Step 02

Choose Your Material i

Each material determines look, price, and turnaround time.

Silver 925 — Lost-wax cast sterling silver. Brilliant, affordable. Price tied to live silver rate (default: 125 EGP/g).

Gold 18K — Lost-wax cast solid gold. Hypoallergenic, tarnish-free, highest value. Price auto-calculated from live 18K gold market rate.

Cobalt Chrome — 3D printed metal (DMLS). Ultra-durable, scratch-resistant. Fixed production cost.

Titanium — 3D printed aerospace-grade titanium. Lightest, fully biocompatible. Fixed production cost.
Step 03

Design Level i

From clean and minimal to masterpiece-level sculpture. Complexity adds to the designer fee and final retail price.

Level 1 — Basic (0%) Standard plain cap. No extra detailing.
Level 2 — Simple (+5%) Basic shape changes or simple cutouts (e.g. window/open face).
Level 3 — Medium (+10%) Texture work or shaped framing (e.g. drip, flame).
Level 4 — Hard (+15%) Intricate manual detail (e.g. flower).
Level 5 — Complex (+25%) Advanced layering or multi-step design (e.g. snake, scorpion).
Level 6 — Master (+40%) Full artistic sculpting, expert-level modeling (e.g. mermaid).
Level 7 — Masterpiece (Special) Maximum complexity — one-of-a-kind commission. Price on consultation.
