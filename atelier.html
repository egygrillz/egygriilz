Select Your Teeth i

Tap each tooth you want covered. Tap again to deselect.

What does this mean? Grillz are custom-fitted caps that sit over your teeth. You choose exactly which teeth get covered — it could be just your two front teeth, a full top row, or any combination. Each tooth is one piece, priced individually.

U = Upper jaw (top teeth)  |  L = Lower jaw (bottom teeth)
Numbers 1–8 count outward from your center front tooth. U1/L1 = your front center tooth. U8/L8 = your back molar.

Step 02

Choose Your Material i

The material determines the look, durability, and turnaround time of your piece.

Silver 925 — Sterling silver. Brilliant, affordable, excellent for intricate designs and iced-out looks. Tarnishes slightly over time but easy to polish.

Gold 18K — Solid 18 karat gold. Hypoallergenic, tarnish-free, and holds its value. The most prestigious option. Price reflects current gold market rate.

Cobalt Chrome — A gunmetal alloy. Extremely scratch-resistant and dense. Gives a distinctive dark silver appearance. Ideal for bold minimal designs.

Titanium — Aerospace-grade titanium. The lightest material we offer, yet stronger than steel. Fully biocompatible (used in dental implants). Can be anodized in vivid permanent colors.
Step 03

Design Style i

From clean and minimal to masterpiece-level sculpture. Choose your aesthetic.

What are design styles? Each style reflects the complexity and craftsmanship involved. Simpler styles have fewer cuts and details. Complex styles involve hours of additional hand-sculpting, pattern work, and artisan labor. The more intricate the design, the higher the craftsmanship fee.

Enamel is a glass-like colored coating fused permanently into the metal at high heat. It adds vivid, lasting color that will not peel or fade.

Basic — Single solid color fill on the surface.

Surface Plating
Yellow · Rose · Black · White · Silver
Finish Color
i
Plating adds a thin layer of precious metal over the surface, changing the color and finish without changing the base material. For example, a Silver 925 base can be plated yellow gold for a gold look at a lower price point. All plating is professionally applied and long-lasting.
