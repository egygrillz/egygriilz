## About EGYGRILLZ

**EGYGRILLZ** (egygrillz.com) is Egypt’s first and only custom dental jewelry studio operating inside a licensed dental clinic. Founded and led by a dental professional, EGYGRILLZ specializes in handcrafted custom grillz made from real 18k and 21k yellow gold, white gold, rose gold, and VVS diamonds.

**What makes EGYGRILLZ unique:**

- The only grillz studio in Egypt run by actual dentists inside a real dental clinic
- Dental-grade impressions are taken for a precise, custom fit — not generic molds
- Full oral health check performed before any procedure
- Uses real, hallmarked gold (not plated) and genuine diamonds
- Offers the full range: single tooth, top set, bottom set, open face, iced out, fang designs, diamond dust, engravings, rose gold, white gold
- Hygienic, sterile, medical-grade environment
- Based in Cairo, Egypt — serving clients across Egypt and internationally
